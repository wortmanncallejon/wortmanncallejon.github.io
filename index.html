About me

Welcome to my website! My name is Felix Wortmann Callejon and I am a graduate student of political science at Departement of Government of the London School of Economics and Political Science. I am interested in the ways institutions influence human behaviour in crisis situations, particularly during the climate crisis. For my research I rely on quantitative methodology - especially causal inference - which I implement in R. Outside of my research, I love to ride my bicycle (preferably in the mountains), play volleyball, cook, and drink espresso.

Research Interests

  • Political Economy
  • Accountability
  • Representation
  • Quantitative Political Methodology
